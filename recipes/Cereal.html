Cereal, the right way

Description

The peanut butter jelly sandwich is a timeless staple of afternoon snack, or whole lunch if you are in my family. For someone who cannot cook very well...this is as good as it gets!

Ingredients

  • Bread (With seeds, whatever that is called)
  • Chunky Peanut Butter
  • Fancy Jelly (or Jam!)
  • Smidgen of Honey
    Steps
  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Place the toasted bread slices on a plate.
  3. Lather one slice with Chunky Peanut Butter and the other with Fancy Jelly.
  4. Add a smidgen of Honey to the middle of either slice, then combine then slices.
