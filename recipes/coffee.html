The Only Acceptable Coffee

Description

Don't talk to me yet, I haven't finished my cup.

Ingredients

  • Folger's Black Silk ("Das it!")
  • Non-tap Water
    Steps
  1. Grab a coffee filter and insert it in the coffee maker.
  2. Scoop about 4 heaping tablespoons of Folger's Black silk into the filter.
  3. Pour 6 cups of water into the coffee maker.
  4. Brew and enjoy.
  5. Add creamer if you want. I will not gatekeep goodness here if all other steps are followed.
