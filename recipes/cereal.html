Cereal, the right way

Description

There have been many arguments and lost friendships regarding the order in which milk is added to cereal. Today we will end the debate.

Ingredients

  • Cereal of choice. I prefer Cheerios at this old age.
  • Oatmilk (stop drinking milk, Anthony. You don't even put anything in it.)
    Steps
  1. Place the bowl on an even surface.
  2. Pour your Cereal of choice gently into the bowl.
  3. Resist the urge to perform these steps out of order.
  4. Pour the Oatmilk into the bowl.
  5. Insert spoon, and enjoy.
